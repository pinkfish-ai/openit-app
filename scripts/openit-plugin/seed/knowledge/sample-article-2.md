# Sample — Granting access to a shared Figma project

> Sample KB article — safe to delete. Replace with your own runbooks as you capture answers.

When a teammate asks for access to a shared Figma workspace or project:

1. **Confirm the exact workspace.** Figma has both **organizations** (top-level) and **projects** (nested). Get the URL the asker is trying to reach.
2. **Check seat availability.** Editor seats cost money; Viewer seats are usually free. Default to Viewer unless they've asked for Edit access and have a clear reason.
3. **Add via the workspace admin panel.** Settings → Members → Invite. Use their work email. Cc the requester so they get the invite confirmation.
4. **Confirm in the ticket.** Reply with "Added you as Viewer to <workspace>. You'll get an email from Figma — accept it within 7 days or the invite expires."

## When to escalate

If the asker is asking for **Edit access on a billable seat** and you don't have approval authority, escalate to the admin. Don't just grant Editor seats by default — most "I need Figma access" requests are satisfied by Viewer.
